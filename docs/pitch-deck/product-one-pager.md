## The Autonomous OS for Smart City Operations

CivicPulse AI is an enterprise-grade platform that provides a real-time "AI brain" for city micro-operations. It transforms city management from a slow, reactive model into a fast, predictive, and autonomous system.

---

### The Problem
Cities worldwide are overwhelmed by thousands of daily "micro-incidents"â€”overflowing trash bins, failed streetlights, water leaks, and traffic bottlenecks. The traditional "wait for a complaint" model is inefficient, costly, and results in poor public service.

### The Solution
CivicPulse AI ingests live sensor data from across the city to create a **Digital Twin**, or a live "health map." Its AI engine then:

1.  **Detects** anomalies in real-time.
2.  **Predicts** failures *before* they happen (e.g., "Trash bin 4A will overflow in 3 hours").
3.  **Acts** by autonomously planning and dispatching (simulated) work orders to the right teams.
